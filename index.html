Cardiology Department

Our Cardiology Department is equipped with the latest technology to diagnose and treat heart conditions. Our team of experienced cardiologists provides comprehensive care for various heart-related issues, including:

  • Coronary artery disease
  • Heart failure
  • Arrhythmias
  • Valve disorders
  • Preventive cardiology

We offer advanced diagnostic procedures such as echocardiography, stress testing, cardiac catheterization, and electrophysiology studies.

Neurology Department

Our Neurology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Our neurologists are experts in treating:

  • Stroke
  • Epilepsy
  • Multiple sclerosis
  • Parkinson's disease
  • Headaches and migraines

We utilize advanced neuroimaging techniques and neurophysiological testing to provide accurate diagnoses and effective treatment plans.

Orthopedics Department

Our Orthopedics Department focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disorders of the musculoskeletal system. Our orthopedic surgeons specialize in:

  • Joint replacement surgery
  • Sports medicine
  • Spine surgery
  • Trauma care
  • Pediatric orthopedics

We offer comprehensive care for bone and joint conditions, from conservative management to advanced surgical interventions.

Pediatrics Department

Our Pediatrics Department provides specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents. Our pediatricians are dedicated to the physical, emotional, and developmental well-being of young patients. Services include:

  • Well-child visits and immunizations
  • Acute illness management
  • Chronic disease management
  • Developmental assessments
  • Adolescent medicine

Our child-friendly environment ensures that young patients feel comfortable and secure during their visits.

Oncology Department

Our Oncology Department provides comprehensive cancer care, from diagnosis to treatment and follow-up. Our multidisciplinary team of oncologists, surgeons, and radiation therapists work together to develop personalized treatment plans. Services include:

  • Medical oncology
  • Radiation oncology
  • Surgical oncology
  • Hematology
  • Cancer screening and prevention

We utilize the latest advancements in cancer treatment to provide the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Gynecology Department

Our Gynecology Department specializes in women's health, focusing on the female reproductive system. Our gynecologists provide comprehensive care for women of all ages. Services include:

  • Routine gynecological exams
  • Family planning
  • Menopause management
  • Gynecological surgery
  • Treatment of reproductive disorders

We are committed to providing compassionate and personalized care to address the unique health needs of women.
